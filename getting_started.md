# Getting Started

## How to Create an Account
Visit our website and click "Sign Up". Enter your email address and choose a password. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox. Complete your profile setup. You are ready to go!

## Supported Browsers
We support the latest two versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Internet Explorer is not supported. For the best experience, we recommend Chrome or Firefox.

## Mobile App Installation
Download our app from the App Store (iOS 13+) or Google Play Store (Android 8.0+). Log in with your existing account credentials. Enable notifications to stay updated on important alerts.

## Keyboard Shortcuts
- Ctrl/Cmd + K: Open search
- Ctrl/Cmd + N: New item
- Ctrl/Cmd + S: Save
- Ctrl/Cmd + Z: Undo
- Escape: Close modal or panel

## How to Contact Support
Live chat is available Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm EST. Email support: support@company.com (response within 24 hours). Phone: 1-800-COMPANY (available for Pro and Enterprise plans only).
