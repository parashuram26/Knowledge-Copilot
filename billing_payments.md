# Billing & Payments

## How to Update Payment Method
Go to Billing > Payment Methods > Add New Card. Enter your card details and click Save. To set as default, click the three dots next to the card and select "Set as Default".

## How to Download an Invoice
Navigate to Billing > Invoice History. Find the invoice you need and click the Download button (PDF). Invoices are available for all transactions in the past 24 months.

## How to Cancel Subscription
Go to Settings > Subscription > Cancel Plan. Select a cancellation reason. Your plan will remain active until the end of the current billing period. You will receive a confirmation email.

## Refund Policy
Refunds are available within 14 days of purchase for annual plans. Monthly plans are non-refundable but you can cancel to avoid future charges. To request a refund, contact billing@company.com with your order number.

## How to Upgrade or Downgrade Plan
Go to Billing > Change Plan. Select your new plan and review the prorated charges. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
