# Technical Support

## How to Clear Cache and Cookies
In Chrome: Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Cmd+Shift+Delete on Mac). Select "All time" as the time range. Check "Cookies and other site data" and "Cached images and files". Click "Clear data". Refresh the page and try again.

## App is Loading Slowly
Check your internet connection speed at speedtest.net. Disable browser extensions that may interfere. Clear your browser cache. Try a different browser. If the problem persists, check our status page at status.company.com.

## How to Export Your Data
Go to Settings > Privacy > Export Data. Select the data types you want to export. Click "Request Export". You will receive an email with a download link within 24 hours. The link expires after 7 days.

## How to Report a Bug
Go to Help > Report a Bug. Describe the issue in detail including steps to reproduce it. Attach screenshots if possible. Our team will investigate and respond within 2 business days.

## API Rate Limits
Free tier: 100 requests per hour. Pro tier: 1,000 requests per hour. Enterprise: Custom limits. If you exceed limits, you will receive a 429 error. Limits reset at the top of each hour.
