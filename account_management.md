# Account Management

## How to Reset Your Password
To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address. You will receive a password reset link within 5 minutes. Click the link and enter a new password (minimum 8 characters, must include one uppercase letter and one number).

## How to Update Your Profile
Navigate to Settings > Profile. You can update your name, email, phone number, and profile picture. Click "Save Changes" when done. Note: Changing your email requires re-verification.

## How to Delete Your Account
To permanently delete your account, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. You will need to confirm your password. This action is irreversible and all data will be removed within 30 days.

## How to Enable Two-Factor Authentication
Go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication. Download an authenticator app (Google Authenticator or Authy). Scan the QR code shown on screen. Enter the 6-digit code to verify setup. Store your backup codes in a safe place.
